# Niche Priority

## Best first niches

### 1. Small agencies
Why: already understand lead lists and may buy fast if priced low.

### 2. Recruiters / staffing shops
Why: direct use for prospecting and candidate/client sourcing.

### 3. Coaches / consultants
Why: often need outbound lists but lack systems.

### 4. Local service businesses
Why: big volume, but may require more explanation.

## Recommendation
Start with small agencies first.
